Safety & Disclaimer

This platform is for educational simulation and interface demonstration only.

Educational Purpose

The diagnostic output is simulated and intended to teach triage logic and interpretation flow.

Not a Medical Diagnosis

Results must not replace physician examination, slit-lamp evaluation, or emergency care pathways.

Data Handling in This Demo

All interactions run locally in the browser. No files are uploaded to a remote server in local demo mode.

Model Limitations

Algorithmic systems can produce false positives/negatives due to image quality, bias, and domain shift.

Clinical Escalation Advice

If severe pain, sudden vision loss, intense photophobia, trauma, or corneal opacity is present, seek urgent ophthalmology care immediately.
